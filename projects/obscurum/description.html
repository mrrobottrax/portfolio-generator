This game was made by me and 4 others during a 4 day student game jam. I can't remeber exactly what the theme of the jam was, something like 'a unique way of interacting with the world'. We chose to make a game very focused on sound design, where the player can only see by poking around with a stick. This was inspired by that lidar Gmod addon that was popular at the time.

  • Development time: 4 days
  • Group size: 5
  • Date: 2022

Tools Used

If you've looked at some of the other group projects, you might be able to guess that we chose Unity as our game engine. I also created the sound effects using Audacity.

Development

The project came along very quickly, mostly due to it's small scope. I did the programming, sound design, level design, and created a couple 3D assets.

The outline effect when poking walls was a challenge. It was my first time programming a shader, and I had to learn HLSL in just a few days. There were also a lot of technical challenges with trying to get the effect to only appear near where you hit.
