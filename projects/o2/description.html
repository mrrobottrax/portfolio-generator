This game was made during a 48 hour game jam. The player explores a mysteriously empty mining facility on an alien moon.

  • Development time: 48 hours
  • Group size: 3
  • Date: 2023

Tools Used

Unity as the game engine, audacity for sound design.

Development

As far as 48 hour jams go, this one went well. I was in charge of sound design, additional programming, and art for the exterior areas.
