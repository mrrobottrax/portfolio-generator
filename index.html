About

Thank you for checking out my portfolio!

I'm currently studying Game Development and Ontario Tech University. I've always had a deep love for creating games and have been programming computers almost my whole life. It's only natural that I would combine the two and start programming games!

Although I love programming, I also enjoy participating in the entire process of game development, from design to art and sound. I've gotten a lot of joy out of the wide variety of skills you can learn when creating games, and am always more than happy to pick up another.

When I started game development all those years ago (in 2013 according to my Scratch account creation date!), I hardly expected it to be such a big part of my life. But as I moved on to more and more capable tools (Unity and Unreal), I started to realize the sheer potential that lies in this industry.

Over the past year, I've become interested in creating my own game engines from scratch, using various programming languages. During this time, I learned more about computer science and software engineering than I had in the 10-ish years since I informally started programming!

Sorry for the long read!

- Andrew Hoult
