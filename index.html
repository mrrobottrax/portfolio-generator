About

Thank you for checking out my portfolio!

I'm currently studying Game Development and Ontario Tech University. I've always had a deep love for creating games and have been programming computers almost my whole life. It's only natural that I would combine the two and start programming games!

Although I love programming, I enjoy every part of game development. My favourite part of making games is the wide variety of skills you get to learn, and I'm always more than happy to pick up another.

When I started game development all those years ago (in 2013 according to my Scratch account creation date), I hardly expected it to be such a big part of my life. But as I moved on to more capable tools such as Unity and Unreal, I started to realize how much depth the medium holds.

Over the past year, I've become interested in creating my own game engines from scratch, using various programming languages. During this time, I've learned more about computer science and software engineering than I had in the 10-ish years since I informally started programming, and am excited to learn even more.

Sorry for the long read!

- Andrew Hoult
